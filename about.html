My Journey

I've been in the CPA and affiliate marketing space for over 8 years now. What started as a side hustle while working a 9-to-5 became my full-time focus - and eventually, my passion for teaching others.

I remember when I made my first $47 commission through an email link. It felt like magic - someone I had never met bought something because of an email I sent. That moment changed everything for me.

Since then, I've:

  • Built email lists of 50,000+ subscribers across multiple niches
  • Generated over $500K in CPA and affiliate revenue
  • Managed campaigns for 7-figure product launches
  • Consulted for major SaaS companies on their affiliate programs
  • Written thousands of emails that actually convert

But honestly? The best part isn't the money. It's the freedom. Email marketing gave me the ability to work from anywhere, build something that scales, and help others do the same.

What I Believe

Value First, Revenue Second

Every email I send is designed to help the reader first. If you provide genuine value, the revenue follows naturally.

Systems Over Motivation

The best results come from building systems that work while you sleep. Email automation is the ultimate lever for passive income.

Honesty Over Hype

I don't believe in "get rich quick" schemes. Building a real business takes time, effort, and genuine skill. I call out BS when I see it.

Community Over Competition

The CPA space is big enough for everyone. We win by helping each other, not by tearing each other down.
