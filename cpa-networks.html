CPA Networks & Programs

Use this page as your operating database for monetization partners. Focus on programs that align with creator-email content and recurring offers where possible.

Important: terms, cookies, and payouts change often. Always confirm on each official partner page before publishing claims.

Kit (ConvertKit) Partner Program

  • Type: SaaS affiliate
  • Commission: recurring (program-specific)
  • Best fit: creator newsletter tutorials
  • Content angle: beginner setup + automation walkthroughs

ActiveCampaign Affiliate

  • Type: SaaS affiliate
  • Commission: flat +/or recurring by plan
  • Best fit: automation and segmentation content
  • Content angle: advanced workflows and case studies

HubSpot Affiliate

  • Type: SaaS / enterprise
  • Commission: often high-ticket one-off tiers
  • Best fit: business/agency audiences
  • Content angle: CRM + email + funnel comparisons

GetResponse Program

  • Type: SaaS affiliate
  • Commission: recurring and bounty options
  • Best fit: webinar + email funnels
  • Content angle: all-in-one marketing stack reviews

PartnerStack Marketplace

  • Type: B2B program marketplace
  • Commission: varies by brand
  • Best fit: scaling content into multi-offer funnels
  • Content angle: software stack comparisons

Impact.com Marketplace

  • Type: affiliate network
  • Commission: varied by merchant
  • Best fit: broad niche expansion after traction
  • Content angle: tool stacks by budget/use case

Approval Readiness Checklist

  • Live domain with clear niche positioning.
  • At least 5 quality articles (not thin affiliate fluff).
  • Transparent affiliate disclosure on site footer/page.
  • About page + contact method.
  • Evidence of traffic source (SEO, social, newsletter).
  • Content that matches the offer audience.
  • No misleading income/earnings promises.
  • Clean, professional landing page UX.
