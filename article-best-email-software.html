The Best Email Marketing Software for Creators in 2026

A no-nonsense comparison of ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, Beehiiv, and Mailchimp. Pick the right tool for your creative business.

Choosing the right email marketing platform is one of the most important decisions you will make for your creative business. The wrong tool can slow you down, cost you money, or make simple tasks frustrating.

Here is the truth: there is no single "best" platform. The right choice depends on your needs, your budget, and where you are in your creator journey.

Which Should You Choose?

Start with Kit if...

You are a creator just starting out. You want something simple that just works. You will appreciate not having to configure dozens of settings.

Choose Beehiiv if...

You are focused purely on newsletters. You want the biggest free tier. You plan to monetize through newsletters directly.

Pick Mailchimp if...

You need advanced features. You have a small team. You are comfortable with a steeper learning curve for more power.

Go ActiveCampaign if...

You need complex automation. You have a sales process. You want machine learning to optimize your campaigns.
