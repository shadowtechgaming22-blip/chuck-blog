7 Email Marketing Mistakes Killing Your Conversions

Stop losing subscribers and money. Here's what you're doing wrong‚Äîand how to fix it.

Most email marketers are leaving money on the table. These 7 mistakes are probably costing you more than you think.

1. No Welcome Sequence

Your new subscribers are most engaged in the first 7 days. If you're not sending a welcome series, you're missing out on up to 10x more conversions.

Fix: Create a 5-email welcome sequence that introduces yourself, delivers your lead magnet, and pitches your main offer.

2. Sending Too Often

Nothing kills a list faster than email overload. Most creators see best results with 1-2 emails per week.

Fix: Audit your sending frequency. Quality beats quantity every time.

3. Weak Subject Lines

If your subject line doesn't stop the scroll, your email never gets opened. Period.

Fix: Use curiosity, urgency, or personal relevance. Examples:

  • ‚Ä¢ "The mistake costing you $1,000/month"
  • ‚Ä¢ "Quick favor..."
  • ‚Ä¢ "Your Tuesday tip"

4. No Call to Action

Every email should have ONE clear action. Don't make your reader guess what you want them to do.

5. Writing Essays

People skim emails. Long blocks of text get deleted.

Fix: Keep emails under 200 words. Use short paragraphs and bullet points.

6. Ignoring Mobile

Over 60% of emails are opened on mobile. If your email isn't mobile-friendly, you're invisible to most of your list.

7. No List Segmentation

Sending the same email to everyone is lazy and ineffective.

Fix: Segment by interest, behavior, or purchase history. Targeted emails convert 2-3x higher.
