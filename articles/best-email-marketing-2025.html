My Recommendation

For most creators starting out, I recommend Beehiiv. The generous free tier (up to 2,500 subscribers), built-in growth tools, and creator-first approach make it the best entry point. Once you're monetizing and need more sophisticated automations, migrating to ConvertKit is seamless.

If you're running a business with multiple products and need CRM functionality, Mailchimp's all-in-one approach saves you from tool sprawl. Just be prepared for a steeper learning curve.

"The best email marketing tool is the one you'll actually use. Start simple, focus on consistency, and upgrade as your needs grow."

Final Thoughts

Email marketing in 2025 is about building genuine connections at scale. The tools have never been better, but they're just enablers. Your voice, consistency, and value to subscribers matter far more than which platform you choose.
