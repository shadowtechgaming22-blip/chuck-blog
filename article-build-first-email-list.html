How to Build Your First Email List from Scratch

A complete step-by-step guide for beginners. No website, no tech skills, no budget required.

Building an email list from scratch can feel overwhelming. You might think you need a website, a blog, hundreds of followers, or money for ads.

You do not need any of that. You need a strategy and you need to take action. This guide will walk you through everything.

A lead magnet is a free resource you give away in exchange for someone's email address. It must be valuable enough that people will actually sign up.

üìù Template

Example: If your audience is podcasters, your lead magnet could be "The Podcast Equipment Checklist for Beginners" or "10 Podcast Scripts You Can Use Today."

3

Set Up Your Landing Page

Your landing page is where people go to sign up for your email list. Kit has built-in landing pages you can use for free.

To create a landing page in Kit:

  1. Go to your Kit dashboard
  2. Click "Landing Pages" in the left menu
  3. Choose a template (keep it simple)
  4. Fill in your headline and description
  5. Upload or describe your lead magnet
  6. Publish and get your URL

Your landing page URL will look something like: yourname.kit.com. You can share this link anywhere.

4

Drive Traffic to Your Page

Now the real work begins. You need to get people to visit your landing page. Here is how to do it without a website:

üê¶ Twitter/X Strategy

Write valuable tweets about your niche. End your best tweets with a call to action to download your free lead magnet.

üí¨ Reddit Strategy

Find subreddits related to your niche. Answer questions, be helpful, and include your link in your bio (not in comments where it looks spammy).

‚ùì Quora Strategy

Answer questions in your niche. At the end of helpful answers, mention that you have a free resource with more tips.

üì± Instagram/TikTok Bio

Update your bio link to your Kit landing page. Create content that makes people want to click.

5

Follow Up With Subscribers

Getting someone to subscribe is only the beginning. Now you need to build the relationship.

üìß Send a Welcome Email Immediately

This is your most important email. Deliver your lead magnet, thank them for subscribing, and introduce yourself.

Your welcome sequence should be 3-5 emails:

  • Email 1: Deliver the lead magnet (send immediately)
  • Email 2: Tell your story and why you care about this topic (2 days later)
  • Email 3: Share your best content or tips (4 days later)
  • Email 4: Introduce a soft sell or affiliate product (7 days later)
  • Email 5: Ask them to follow you on social media (10 days later)

What to Expect in Month 1

Week 1-2: Set Up

Choose platform, create lead magnet, build landing page. This is the foundation.

Week 2-3: Launch

Share your landing page everywhere. Post on social media, Reddit, Twitter. Tell everyone you know.

Week 3-4: Engage

Send your welcome sequence. Start building relationships. Aim for 10-50 subscribers this month.

Month 2+: Grow

Keep posting. Add new strategies. The compound effect kicks in and growth accelerates.
