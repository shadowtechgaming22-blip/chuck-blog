How to Build a CPA Funnel from Scratch

A complete step-by-step guide to building a CPA marketing funnel that converts. From traffic to landing page to affiliate offer.

A CPA funnel is the system that takes a stranger from never hearing about you to clicking your affiliate link. Every part matters. Let me walk you through building one from scratch.

The 5 Stages of a CPA Funnel

Stage 1: Traffic Source

Where are your ideal customers hanging out?

  • Organic search (SEO)
  • Social media (LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok)
  • Paid ads (start with small budget)
  • Email list (if you have one)

Stage 2: Lead Magnet

Something free and valuable that captures their email address:

  • Free guide or ebook
  • Checklist or template
  • Video course
  • Webinar or training

Stage 3: Landing Page

The page that captures their email. One goal: get the conversion.

Stage 4: Welcome Sequence

Series of emails that deliver the lead magnet and build trust.

Stage 5: Affiliate Offer

The CPA offer that matches your audience's problem.

Step-by-Step Funnel Build

Step 1: Choose Your Niche

Pick something you know. Something with problems you can solve. Something with affiliate offers available.

Step 2: Find CPA Offers

Join networks like ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, or PartnerStack. Pick offers that match your niche.

Step 3: Create Lead Magnet

Something quick to create. A PDF checklist, template, or short video. Solve one specific problem.

Step 4: Build Landing Page

Use Carrd, Leadpages, or build with HTML. Keep it simple. Headline, benefits, form, CTA.

Step 5: Set Up Email Sequence

3-5 emails. Deliver value first. Build trust. Then naturally introduce the offer.

Step 6: Drive Traffic

Start with one traffic source. Test and optimize. Scale what works.

Simple CPA Funnel Example

Niche: Remote Work Productivity

  • Lead Magnet: "Remote Work Toolkit" (Notion templates)
  • Landing Page: Carrd page with opt-in form
  • Email 1: Here is your toolkit
  • Email 2: My favorite remote work tools
  • Email 3: Review of [Tool] with affiliate link
  • Offer: [Tool] affiliate link

Common Funnel Mistakes

  • Too many CTAs on landing page
  • No follow-up emails
  • Promoting wrong offer to audience
  • Not testing variations
  • Giving up too early

Tools to Build Your Funnel

  • Carrd: Simple one-page sites ($19/year)
  • Kit: Email marketing and automation
  • ConvertKit: Great for creators
  • Leadpages: Conversion-focused templates

Final Thoughts

Start simple. One lead magnet, one landing page, one email sequence. Get one part working before adding complexity. Then scale and optimize.
