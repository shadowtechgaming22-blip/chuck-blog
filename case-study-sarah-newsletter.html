Sarah's Newsletter

Solopreneur · Email Marketing

$2,340/month

From 0 to 2,300 subscribers to $2,340/month in 8 months — here's exactly how she did it.

When Sarah first started her newsletter in January 2025, she had zero subscribers, zero audience, and no idea if anyone would care about productivity tips for remote workers. Eight months later, she was earning $2,340/month in passive affiliate income — all from an email list she built from scratch.

The Beginning: Why She Started

Sarah was working as a project manager at a tech startup. She loved her job, but she wanted something of her own — a way to build something that didn't depend on a salary.

"I spent 2 hours every day on LinkedIn consuming content about remote work. I realized I could create that content instead of just consuming it," she told us.

Month 1-2: Finding Her Niche

Sarah didn't just start writing about "productivity." She got specific:

  • Niche: Productivity tips specifically for remote workers who use Notion
  • Format: One short email per week, 5 minutes to read max
  • Lead magnet: A free "Remote Work Toolkit" — Notion templates, daily planning checklists, Zoom fatigue tips

Month 3-4: The Traffic Strategy That Worked

Sarah tried many things to drive traffic. Here's what actually moved the needle:

✓ What Worked:

  • • LinkedIn carousels with productivity tips (3x per week)
  • • Every carousel linked to her lead magnet
  • • Commented on 10+ posts daily in her niche
  • • Posted in 3 relevant LinkedIn groups

✗ What Didn't Work:

  • • Twitter/X posting (no traction)
  • • Reddit posts (mostly banned/spam filtered)
  • • Guest posting on other newsletters (too competitive)

Month 5-6: Growing the List

By month 5, Sarah had 800 subscribers. She started focusing on engagement:

  • Replied to every single email reply personally
  • Created a "Best replies" highlight in her newsletter
  • Asked one question per email to encourage responses
  • Her open rate stayed at 68% — extremely high

Month 7-8: The First Affiliate Dollar

Sarah joined the Kit (ConvertKit) partner program and started including her affiliate link in every email:

  • First month: $47 in affiliate commissions
  • Second month: $312
  • Third month: $890
  • Fourth month: $2,340

The key? She only promoted tools she actually used and wrote detailed comparison posts about them.

Her Email Strategy

The 90/10 Rule

Sarah sends 4 value-first emails for every 1 promotional email. Her subscribers trust her because she consistently provides value without pitching.

Her Welcome Sequence

Day 0: Deliver the toolkit → Day 2: One quick win → Day 5: Her story → Day 14: First tool recommendation (with affiliate link)

Key Takeaways

  1. Get specific: "Remote work productivity" > "Productivity"
  2. One channel at a time: She mastered LinkedIn before trying anything else
  3. Reply to everyone: Engagement builds trust and loyalty
  4. Promote what you use: Authenticity converts
  5. Be patient: It took 6 months to see real income

Where She Is Now

Sarah now has 4,100+ subscribers and earns $3,200/month primarily from affiliate commissions. She's also launched a paid course called "Remote Work Newsletter Blueprint" that brings in an additional $800/month.

She still replies to every email personally. "That's the secret sauce," she says. "Treat your subscribers like friends, not metrics."
