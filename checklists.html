Execution Checklists

Use these lists every week so the funnel keeps moving: content shipped, emails sent, links tracked, and revenue reviewed.

1) New Article Publish Checklist

  • ☐ Keyword intent matches article title and intro.
  • ☐ Problem, solution, and CTA are clear above the fold.
  • ☐ At least one internal link to a related post.
  • ☐ Affiliate link includes clear disclosure near CTA.
  • ☐ Meta title + description filled (SEO basics done).
  • ☐ Hero image + social preview image present.
  • ☐ Added to homepage "Latest Articles" section.
  • ☐ Shared in newsletter and one social channel.

2) Affiliate Offer QA Checklist

  • ☐ Offer still active and landing page works.
  • ☐ Commission and terms verified this month.
  • ☐ Cookie duration noted in your tracking sheet.
  • ☐ UTM parameters added to each link variant.
  • ☐ One primary CTA + one fallback CTA in article.
  • ☐ Link tested in incognito and mobile.
  • ☐ Comparison claims are accurate and fair.
  • ☐ Disclosure visible on post + footer.

3) Email Sequence Checklist

  • ☐ Welcome email delivers lead magnet instantly.
  • ☐ Day 2 email gives one tactical quick win.
  • ☐ Day 3 email bridges naturally into offer.
  • ☐ Every email has one clear CTA.
  • ☐ Personalization tokens tested (no broken variables).
  • ☐ Mobile preview checked before sending.
  • ☐ Unsubscribe link and sender info are compliant.
  • ☐ Sequence analytics reviewed after 7 days.

4) Weekly Growth Review

  • ☐ Published at least 1 high-intent article.
  • ☐ Sent at least 1 value-first email broadcast.
  • ☐ Checked top 10 pages by clicks and scroll depth.
  • ☐ Logged top CTR affiliate links by article.
  • ☐ Rewrote underperforming headlines/CTAs.
  • ☐ Pruned low-impact tasks and doubled down on winners.
  • ☐ Updated next-week content plan.
  • ☐ Captured one key learning in notes.

30-Day Operating Rhythm

Week 1

Research 10 low-competition keywords + outline 2 posts.

Week 2

Publish 2 posts + set up / refine welcome sequence.

Week 3

A/B test CTA blocks and subject lines.

Week 4

Performance review + prioritize next-month content bets.
